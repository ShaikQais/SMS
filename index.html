The department objective is to motivate all students to actively participate in Physical Sports in order to develop spirit of Sportsmanship, Teamwork and Leadership among the students. Participation in sports also helps promote the Physical and Mental Wellbeing of the students. The department encourages student to compete and participate in various sports disciplines at the university, zonal, state and national levels.

The College Sports Management System is a comprehensive software solution designed to streamline and enhance the management of college sports programs. It provides a centralized platform for coaches, administrators, athletes, and other stakeholders to efficiently handle various aspects of sports management, including team scheduling, athlete registration, performance tracking, communication, and financial management. The system features an intuitive and user-friendly interface accessible through both web and mobile applications, ensuring convenient access for users on different devices.
